Upload your shp and dbf file along with optional prj and shx files and shpworld will convert it to a geojson.

Hit ShpWorld's RESTful endpoint if web interfaces aren't your style:

curl -is -F shp=@newcounts.shp -F dbf=@newcounts.dbf -F prj=@newcounts.prj -F shx=@newcounts.shx localhost:8080/shptogeo.json
